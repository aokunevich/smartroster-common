Battlefront: Shattered Dominion 🛠 Tournament Format The event is a one-day tournament with 3 rounds, following Matched Play rules (10th Edition). Each game is played at 1500 points using the Pariah Nexus mission pack. Pairings for Round 1 will be random. Rounds 2 and 3 will be Swiss-style based on results. 🕒 Schedule (tentative) Stage Time Check-In & Setup 10:00 – 10:30 Round 1 10:30 – 13:00 Lunch Break 13:00 – 13:30 Round 2 13:30 – 16:00 Round 3 16:15 – 18:45 Awards & Wrap-up 19:00 📋 Army Lists Submit one 1500pt list using the rules of Matched Play. Lists must be uploaded via SmartRoster: https://smartroster.club Lists can be submitted via Battlescribe, the Warhammer App, or as a readable PDF. Only your faction stays locked for the event — small adjustments to the list are not allowed after submission. 🎯 Missions All games will use missions from the Pariah Nexus pack with fixed setups. Sample Mission Set (subject to change): Round 1: Take and Hold / Stalwarts / Crucible of Battle Round 2: The Ritual / Inspired Leadership / Hammer and Anvil Round 3: Terraform / Smoke and Mirrors / Dawn of War 📬 Communication & Organization All announcements and pairings will be posted in our official Discord server: 👉 https://discord.gg/hEUbeTvhZn Tournament channel: #tournament Post questions, find your opponents, and share battle reports there. 📝 Scoring & Results Report your results via SmartRoster after each round. Scoring System: 🥇 Win = 3 points 🤝 Draw = 1 point ❌ Loss = 0 points Secondary metrics (Victory Points, opponent strength, etc.) will be used for tie-breaking. 🏆 Awards After Round 3, prizes will be awarded for: 🥇 1st Place – Champion of the Day 🥈 2nd Place – Strongest Runner-up 🖌 Best Painted Unit – Community-voted (optional submission in #hobby) 📸 Reports & Community Share pictures, quick summaries, or full narrative battle reports in #battle-reports. Show off painted miniatures in #hobby — even during the event!
